[[Partikel]]

absolute negation (complete negation)
	•	[[nicht]] – not
	•	[[nie]] / [[niemals]] – never
	•	[[nichts]] – nothing
	•	[[kein]] / keine / keinen… – no, none

partial or graded negation

(these soften or modify the negation)
	•	[[kaum]] – hardly, barely
	•	[[wenig]] – little, hardly any
	•	[[selten]] – rarely
	•	[[noch nicht]] – not yet
	•	[[nicht mehr]] – no longer
	•	[[nicht unbedingt]] – not necessarily
	•	[[nicht wirklich]] – not really
	•	[[keineswegs]] / [[keinesfalls]] – by no means

negative pronouns & adverbs (negation + indefinite meaning)
	•	[[nirgends]] / [[nirgendwo]] – nowhere
	•	[[niemand]] / [[keiner]] – nobody, no one
	•	[[weder, noch]] … – neither … nor …

negative modal particles (subtle negation or restriction)
	•	[[wohl kaum]] – probably not
	•	[[so gut wie nicht]] – as good as not
	•	[[allenfalls nicht]] – at best not,
