 [[Partikel]]

neutral or softening
	•	[[doch]] – adds emphasis, contradiction, or reassurance (komm doch mit! – just come along!)
	•	[[eben]] – expresses inevitability, resignation (dann ist es eben so – well, that’s just how it is)
	•	[[halt]] – similar to eben, but more colloquial (dann mach’s halt! – then just do it!)
	•	[[ja]] – makes a statement sound more obvious or self-evident (das weißt du ja! – you know that after all!)
	•	[[schon]] – reassuring or conceding something (das wird schon klappen! – it’ll be fine!)

intensifying or emphasising
	•	[[aber]] – intensifies a statement (das ist aber schön! – that is REALLY nice!)
	•	[[auch]] – adds emphasis, often with a bit of exasperation (du bist auch unmöglich! – you’re just impossible!)
	•	[[bloß]] – intensifies a warning or wish (mach das bloß nicht! – don’t you dare do that!)
	•	[[denn]] – softens questions, making them more conversational (was machst du denn? – so what are you doing?)
	•	[[eigentlich]] – signals curiosity or hedging (was willst du eigentlich? – what do you actually want?)
	•	[[gar]] – intensifies negation or surprise (das ist gar nicht wahr! – that’s not true at all!)
	•	[[nur]] – adds urgency or intensifies imperatives (sag mir nur die Wahrheit! – just tell me the truth!)
	•	[[ruhig]] – makes imperatives sound more relaxed (mach das ruhig! – go ahead and do it, no worries!)
	•	[[wirklich]] – strengthens sincerity or contrast (bist du wirklich sicher? – are you REALLY sure?)

doubt, impatience, surprise
	•	[[etwa]] – suggests suspicion or concern (hast du etwa vergessen? – don’t tell me you forgot?)
	•	[[wohl]] – implies speculation or softens a statement (er hat es wohl vergessen – i guess he forgot),
