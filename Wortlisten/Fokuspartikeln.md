[[Partikel]]

Fokuspartikeln are those little german words that modify focus or contrast in a sentence, often emphasising or restricting elements. here’s a comprehensive list:

restriktive (restrictive) fokuspartikeln

(these limit or narrow the scope of what is said)
	•	[[allein]] – solely
	•	[[ausschließlich]] – exclusively
	•	[[bloß]] – just, merely
	•	[[einzig]] – only
	•	[[erst]] – only (temporal nuance, meaning “not before”)
	•	[[lediglich]] – merely
	•	[[mehr]] als – more than
	•	[[mindestens]] – at least
	•	[[nur]] – only
	•	[[schlicht]] (und ergreifend) – simply
	•	[[weniger]] als – less than

additive (additive) fokuspartikeln

(these add elements to the sentence, often meaning “also” or “even”)
	•	[[auch]] – also, too
	•	[[außerdem]] – besides
	•	[[ebenfalls]] – likewise
	•	[[gleichfalls]] – likewise
	•	[[noch]] – still, yet
	•	[[selbst]] – even
	•	[[sogar]] – even

kontrastive (contrastive) fokuspartikeln

(these establish contrast between elements)
	•	([[aber]]) immerhin – (but) at least
	•	[[dagegen]] – in contrast
	•	[[hingegen]] – on the other hand
	•	[[jedoch]] – however
	•	[[nur]] – only (can be contrastive depending on context)
	•	[[stattdessen]] – instead

skalierende (scaling) fokuspartikeln

(these indicate a scale of intensity or a hierarchy of elements)
	•	[[am allermeisten]] – most of all
	•	[[besonders]] – particularly
	•	[[genau]] – exactly
	•	[[gerade]] – especially, just
	•	[[insbesondere]] – especially
	•	[[mindestens]] – at least
	•	[[noch]] nicht einmal – not even
	•	[[schon]] – already (can also be a modal particle)
	•	[[sogar]] – even
	•	[[zumindest]] – at least
