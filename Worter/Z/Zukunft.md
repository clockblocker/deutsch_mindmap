Additional Notes:
- Often used in philosophical and strategic contexts
- Represents potential and possibility
- Can refer to personal, societal, or temporal future

Beispiele:
1. "Die Zukunft gehört den Mutigen." (The future belongs to the brave.)
2. "Wir müssen in die Zukunft investieren." (We must invest in the future.)

Grammatical Usage:
- Typically used with definite article
- Can be modified by adjectives
- Often the object of prepositions and verbs related to planning or anticipation
