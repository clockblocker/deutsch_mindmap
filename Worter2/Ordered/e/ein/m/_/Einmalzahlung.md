- [[Einmalzahlung]] *(the most direct, boring, and precise term)*
- [[Zuschuss]] *(implies it’s supportive, but not necessarily one-time (can be contextual))*
- [[Geldleistung]] *(generic term, dry, bureaucratic)*
- [[Beihilfe]] *(often used in welfare or social policy contexts)*
- finanzielle [[Zuwendung]] *(sounds more institutional, like it’s coming from a state or fund)*
- [[Förderbetrag]] *(if it’s meant to promote/support some goal (e.g. birth rate))*
