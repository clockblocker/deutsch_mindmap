1. standard coordinating conjunctions (most common)
	•	[[und]] – and
	•	[[oder]] – or
	•	[[aber]] – but
	•	[[denn]] – because (only used in main clauses)
	•	[[sondern]] – but rather
	•	[[doch]] – but, however

2. alternative or less common coordinating conjunctions
	•	[[bzw]]. (beziehungsweise) – or rather, respectively
	•	[[doch]] – but, still
	•	[[jedoch]] – however (sometimes behaves like a sentence adverb)
	•	[[allein]] – but, however (very formal, literary)

3. correlative coordinating conjunctions (used in pairs)
	•	[[entweder_oder|entweder]] … [[oder|entweder_oder]] … – either … or …
	•	[[weder_noch|weder]] … [[weder_noch|noch]] … – neither … nor …
	•	[[sowohl]] … [[als auch]] … – both … and …
	•	[[nicht nur]] … [[sondern auch]] … – not only … but also …

4. borderline cases (hybrid usage)
	•	[[also]] – so, therefore (functions like an adverb but often links clauses)
	•	[[deshalb]] / [[deswegen]] / [[daher]] – therefore (similar to “also”)
	•	[[trotzdem]] – nevertheless
	•	[[deswegen]] – therefore
	•	[[daher]] hence
